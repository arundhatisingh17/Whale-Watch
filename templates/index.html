Stablecoins move the market — just not the way you'd expect. A stablecoin like USDC or USDT is pegged to the US dollar, so its own price barely moves, unlike volatile assets such as ETH or BTC. That's why traders park capital in stablecoins to hedge — and why a sudden, large stablecoin transfer is often a whale getting into position.
